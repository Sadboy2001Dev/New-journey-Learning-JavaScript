<h1>Operators in JavaScript</h1>

The chapter III is reserved to operators: Arithmetics, relacionals, logical and comparison.

Arithmetics operators such as + (addition), - (subtract), * (multiplication) , / (divison) and  % (modulo)</br>

Relacionals operators such as ==, ===, !=, !=== </br>

Logical operators such as && (AND), || (OR) and ! (NOT) </br>

Comparison operators such as > (greater than), < (less than), >= (greater than or equal) and <= (less than or equal).
